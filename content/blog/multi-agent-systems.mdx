# Why Multi-Agent Systems Will Replace Traditional AI Pipelines

Traditional AI pipelines follow:

Input → Model → Output

But enterprise systems require:

- Validation
- Retry logic
- Guardrails
- Multi-agent collaboration

Multi-agent orchestration enables structured, measurable AI systems.
